Fall Concert

Sunday, December 10, 2023
2:00 p.m.
Lansing Middle School

Aaron Burgess, Music Director
Reuben Foley, Assistant Conductor

Program

  • Giuseppe Verdi: La forza del destino
  • Gustav Holst: St. Paul's Suite, Op. 29
    • conducted by Reuben Foley
  • Gustav Mahler: Um Mitternacht, movement 4 from Rükert-Lieder
  • Peter Ilyich Tchaikovsky: Romeo and Juliet: Fantasy-Overture

Suggested $7 donation at the door. Children admitted free.

This project is made possible with funds from the Statewide Community Regrants Program, a regrant program of the New York State Council on the Arts with the support of the Office of the Governor and the New York State Legislature and administered by the Community Arts Partnership of Tompkins County.

Interested in playing in the ICO? E-mail us at personnel@ithacacommunityorchestra.org (introduce a bit about yourself and experience with your instrument).
