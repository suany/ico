Spring Concert

Saturday, April 27, 2019
4:00 p.m.
at the Hangar Theatre

Madeline Schatz-Harris, Music Director
Aaron Burgess, Assistant Conductor

Program

  • Vivaldi: Spring, from The Four Seasons
    • Sophia Werner, violin
  • Beethoven: Symphony No. 6 (Pastorale), movement 1
  • Smetana: The Moldau
    • conducted by Aaron Burgess
  • INTERMISSION
  • Wieniawski: Polonaise Brillante
    • Sophia Werner, violin
  • Rossini: William Tell Overture

There is a suggested $7 donation at the door. Children admitted free.

This program is made possible in part with funds from:
  • The Decentralization Program, a regrant program of the New York State Council on the Arts with the support of Governor Andrew Cuomo and the New York State Legislature and administered by the Community Arts Partnership of Tompkins County;
  • The Hess Legacy Fund of the Community Foundation of Tompkins County.
