Public In-person Rehearsals

Madeline Schatz-Harris, Music Director
Aaron Burgess, Assistant Conductor

Join us as members of the Ithaca Community Orchestra meet in person for the first time since March of 2020.
  • Wednesday, June 9, 2021, Myers Park, Pavilion G:
    • Winds: Little Symphony for Winds by Schubert
    • Brass: 12 Kleine Stücke by Ludwig Maurer
  • Wednesday, June 16, 2021, Myers Park, Pavilions D and G:
    • Strings: Brandenburg Concerto #3 by Bach
    • Winds: Little Symphony for Winds by Schubert
    • Brass: 12 Kleine Stücke by Ludwig Maurer
  • Wednesday, June 23, 2021, Myers Park, Pavilion D:
    • Strings: Brandenburg Concerto #3 by Bach

Suggested $7 donation at the door. Children admitted free.
Free reception following the concert.

This program is made possible in part with funds from: The Decentralization Program, a regrant program of the New York State Council on the Arts with the support of Governor Andrew Cuomo and the New York State Legislature and administered by the Community Arts Partnership of Tompkins County;
