Ford Hall Concert Parking

(Also available: Printable PDF Version)

Parking is free at Lot J and Lot F on evenings and weekends.

LOT J is closest to the venue. From the traffic circle, drive uphill until you reach a stop sign, take a slight right uphill and continue under the arch to Lot J. Follow the enclosed walkway over to the elevator and either take that down to the Ford Hall Lobby (L), or take the stairs down to the 4th floor.

LOT F is a good second choice. Park towards the north (uphill) end, and walk along Conservatory Drive 500 feet to the main entrance of the Whalen Center. Go upstairs to the Ford Hall lobby.
