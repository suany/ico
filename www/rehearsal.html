Rehearsal Information

The ICO meets every Wednesday during the school year from 7:00-9:00 p.m. at the First Congregational Church in Cayuga Heights (309 Highland Road). All instrumentalists are encouraged to inquire as we have openings in winds, brass, strings and percussion. For more information, send an inquiry to personnel@ithacacommunityorchestra.org (introduce a bit about yourself and experience with your instrument).
