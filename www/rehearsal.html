Rehearsal Information

The ICO meets every Wednesday during the school year from 7:00-9:00 p.m. at the First Congregational Church in Cayuga Heights (309 Highland Road).

Proof of vaccination will be required of all ICO members and guests attending rehearsal, and no one will be admitted without providing proof of vaccination. For these purposes, being vaccinated is defined as having received all doses in the primary series and at least one booster as recommended. Use of masks and bell covers will be left to the discretion of the individual players. This COVID policy is subject to change to reflect the strictest current guidance at the national, state or county level.

All instrumentalists are encouraged to inquire as we have openings in winds, brass, strings and percussion. For more information, send an inquiry to personnel@ithacacommunityorchestra.org (introduce a bit about yourself and experience with your instrument).
